About

Sushi Time Co. is a new clothing line that focuses on silkscreened designs on fabric goods such as t-shirts, hats, bandanas, etc. Setting apart Sushi Time Co. is the emphasis on using the fabric goods as a medium for artistic creation and experimentation. With this idea in mind, Sushi Time Co. blends together elements of graphic design, illustration, and Japanese aesthetics. Below you'll find our most current collection.
